### AI Response Summary
Of course. Forcing a default score on users who don't know it can be confusing, and allowing them to opt-in makes the process much clearer. I've updated the application to include this feature.

1.  **Opt-In Checkbox:** A new checkbox labeled "I know my credit score" has been added. The credit score slider, number input, and provider dropdown are now disabled by default.
2.  **User-Controlled Entry:** To enter a score, you must first check the box, which enables the input fields.
